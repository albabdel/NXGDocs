# Operator Documentation

Welcome to the **Operator** documentation for NXGEN GCXONE.

As an Operator, you monitor live feeds, respond to alarms, and manage day-to-day operations.

## Key Responsibilities

### Live Monitoring
- Monitor live video feeds from multiple devices
- Track real-time system events and alerts
- Maintain situational awareness across sites

### Alarm Response
- Acknowledge and respond to alarms
- Escalate critical incidents
- Document alarm responses and outcomes

### Video Playback
- Review recorded footage for incident investigation
- Create video clips for evidence or training
- Export video for reporting purposes

### Incident Management
- Create incident reports
- Communicate with stakeholders
- Follow standard operating procedures
