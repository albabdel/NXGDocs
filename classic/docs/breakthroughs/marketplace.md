## See It in Action

MarketPlace enables instant service activation and revenue expansion across GCXONE deployments:

### Scenario 1: Mid-Contract Premium Service Upsell

A sales manager closes a deal to add AI-powered people counting analytics to an existing retail client's 25-store deployment. With MarketPlace:
1. The sales manager logs into GCXONE during the client meeting
2. Navigates to MarketPlace, selects "AI People Counting," and reviews pricing: €15/camera/month
3. Uses the subscription interface to select all 25 retail sites and their 200 cameras total
4. Clicks "Activate Feature" - the system confirms: "Monthly cost increase: €3,000"
5. Within 15 minutes, all 200 cameras have AI analytics enabled and are generating foot traffic reports
6. The client receives an email with access credentials and a link to their new analytics dashboard

The transformation: Service activation time drops from 7-10 days to 15 minutes. The sales team closes €36,000 in annual recurring revenue during the meeting instead of weeks later. IT avoids 50+ hours of manual configuration work, and billing is automatically updated with zero administrative overhead.

### Scenario 2: Seasonal Feature Deployment for Events

- After the event, the administrator deactivates the feature to stop recurring charges
- Total billable service: €180 for 3 days across 12 cameras

The measurable impact: The provider generates €180 in margin with 5 minutes of work (€2,160/hour effective rate). The client gets exactly what they need without long-term commitment. This "feature-as-a-service" model unlocks 12+ similar upsells per year, adding €25,000+ in annual revenue that didn't exist before MarketPlace.

## ⚙️ How to Activate MarketPlace

This section explains how to browse, subscribe, and assign features within the MarketPlace.

### Prerequisites

Before using MarketPlace, ensure you have:
- [ ] Administrator access to GCXONE with billing permissions
- [ ] Valid payment method or credit balance in your account
- [ ] Appropriate customer and site hierarchy established

### Activation Steps

#### Step 1: Access the MarketPlace
1. Log into GCXONE as an administrator.
2. Navigate to the **MarketPlace** icon in the sidebar.
3. Browse the available categories (e.g., Analytics, Storage, Utilities).

#### Step 2: Select a Feature
1. Click on a feature to view its capabilities and pricing (e.g., Time Sync or Pulse View).
2. Review the subscription terms and monthly cost per app/site.
3. Click **Activate Feature**.

#### Step 3: Manage Subscriptions
1. Navigate to **Subscription Management**.
2. Search for the specific customers or sites where you want to enable the feature.
3. Use the checkboxes to select the target entities.

#### Step 4: Confirm & Save
1. Review the "Save Subscription?" confirmation dialog.
2. Verify the estimated increase in your monthly cost.
3. Click **Yes** to proceed.

#### Step 5: Verify Deployment
1. Navigate to a site where the feature was enabled.
2. Verify that the new functionality (e.g., a new widget or settings tab) is visible.
3. Check the **Subscription History** for a record of the activation.

## 🚀 Adoption Guide

Successfully leveraging MarketPlace requires aligning technical capabilities with your sales and service strategy.

### Phase 1: Planning (Week 1)

#### Define Your Service Packages
- **Offerings:** Identify which MarketPlace features (e.g., AI Analytics) you will upsell to your clients.
- **Pricing:** Determine your markup and billing model for premium documentation/services.
- **Sales Training:** Brief the sales team on the value proposition of new instant-on features.

### Phase 2: Internal Pilot (Week 2)
- Activate a premium feature for a small subset of internal sites.
- Document the activation time and any configuration hurdles.
- Verify the feature's impact on operational efficiency.

### Phase 3: Customer Rollout (Week 2-3)
- Launch a "Premium Service Tier" to a select group of beta customers.
- Use MarketPlace subscription logs as proof of service for billing.
- Gather feedback on the perceived value of the new features.

### Phase 4: Scaling (Week 4+)
- Gradually roll out MarketPlace features across the entire customer base.
- Standardize the "Subscription Assignment" workflow for all new site onboardings.
- Monitor the 2x revenue-per-site target.

### Best Practices
- **DO** use the "Select All" feature carefully to avoid unexpected billing spikes.
- **DO** verify the feature's prerequisites before activating (e.g., specific camera models for AI).
- **DO** review your subscription summary monthly to clean up unused features.
- **DON'T** activate features on sites without notifying the customer of the cost change.
- **DON'T** share billing permissions with non-administrative staff.
