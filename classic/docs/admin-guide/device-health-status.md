# Device Health Status

Monitor the operational status and connectivity of your devices in real-time.

## Overview

The Device Health Status feature provides an at-a-glance view of which devices are online, offline, or experiencing issues. This allows for proactive maintenance and rapid troubleshooting.

## Health Status Indicators

- **Online (Green)**: The device is communicating normally with GCXONE.
- **Offline (Red)**: The device has lost connectivity or heartbeat.
- **Warning (Yellow/Orange)**: The device is experiencing intermittent issues or high latency.

## Monitoring Health
- **Configuration App**: View the status of all devices at the site level.
- **Talos Dashboard**: Monitor health alerts and automated system events.
- **Heartbeat Signals**: Most devices send periodic signals to GCXONE to confirm their availability.

## Troubleshooting Offline Devices
If a device shows as offline:
1. Check the local network and internet connectivity at the site.
2. Verify power status to the device.
3. Check the "Health Monitoring" logs for specific error codes or disconnection timestamps.
