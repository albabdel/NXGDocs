# Getting Started with GCXONE

Welcome to the **GCXONE** documentation. GCXONE is a fully managed alarm management platform designed to automate repetitive tasks, ensure reliable alarm transmission, and provide seamless integration with IoT devices.

## What is GCXONE?

GCXONE acts as the central nervous system for your security and building automation infrastructure. It connects devices, processes alarms using intelligent workflows, and notifies the right people at the right time.

### Key Features

*   **Universal Connectivity**: Connect any device via standard protocols (SIA, CID, MQTT, REST).
*   **Automated Workflows**: Define logic to handle alarms automatically (e.g., "If fire alarm, call fire department").
*   **Role-Based Access**: Granular permissions for Admins, Operators, and Installers.
*   **Real-time Dashboard**: Monitor your entire fleet of devices in real-time.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

1.  **GCXONE Account**: You should have received an invitation email to activate your account.
2.  **Supported Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions).
3.  **Network Access**: Ensure your firewall allows outbound connections to `*.gcxone.com` on port 443.

## First Steps

### 1. Log in to the Console
Navigate to [https://console.gcxone.com](https://console.gcxone.com) and log in with your credentials.

### 2. Configure your Profile
Go to **Settings > Profile** to set your time zone and notification preferences.

### 3. Connect your First Device
Follow the [Installer Guide](/docs/roles/installer) to provision your first gateway or device.
