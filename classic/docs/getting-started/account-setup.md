# Account Setup

Follow these steps to set up your GCXONE account.

## Creating an Account

1.  Navigate to the GCXONE login page.
2.  Click **Register**.
3.  Fill in your organization details and administrator contact information.
4.  Verify your email address.

## Initial Configuration

Once logged in, you will be guided through the initial setup wizard to:
- Add your first site.
- Connect devices.
- Invite team members.
