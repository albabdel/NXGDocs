# 🏁 First-Time Login & Setup

Welcome to NXGEN GCXONE! This tutorial will guide you through the initial steps of setting up your account, organization, and first site.

## 🏗️ Step-by-Step Configuration

<Steps>

### 1. Account Registration
Navigate to the [GCXONE Portal](https://nxgen.cloud) and select **Register**.
1. Enter your personal details (Name, Email, Contact).
2. Define your **Organization Name**. This will be used for all reporting and billing.
3. Select your setup path:
    - **Existing Camera:** For integrating your current IP infrastructure.
    - **Instant Setup:** For an immediate demo experience without hardware.

### 2. Verify and Set Password
Check your email for a registration confirmation.
1. Click the **Password Reset** link in the email.
2. Set a secure password following the platform requirements.
3. Log in using your new credentials at the portal home page.

### 3. Configure User Roles
Before adding customers, define who has access to what.
1. Navigate to **Settings** → **User Roles**.
2. Click **Configure New Role** to add custom permissions (e.g., "Night Shift Operator").
3. Use the **Users** tab to invite team members and assign these roles.

### 4. Build Your Hierarchy
GCXONE uses a 3-tier model: **Customer → Site → Device**.
1. **Create Customer:** Enter the client's business details and contact info.
2. **Add Site:** under the customer, create a physical location (e.g., "Berlin Warehouse").
3. **Add Device:** Under the site, click **Add New** to register your hardware.

### 5. Device Discovery
Connect your cameras to the platform instantly.
1. Select the **Manufacturer** (e.g., Hikvision, Dahua).
2. Enter the IP address and credentials.
3. Click **Discover**. The system will automatically map the camera channels and features.

</Steps>

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- **Descriptive Naming:** Always use specific names for sites (e.g., "Exit Gate 04" instead of "Site 1").
- **Least Privilege:** Assign roles based on actual job functions to maintain security.
- **Instant Setup:** Use the demo mode first to familiarize yourself with the UI before deploying physical hardware.

---

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

- **No Email?** Check your spam folder or ensure `no-reply@nxgen.cloud` is whitelisted.
- **Discovery Failed?** Verify that the camera's ONVIF or API port is open on your firewall.
- **Login Issues?** Clear your browser cache or try an incognito window.
