# First-Time Login & Setup

Welcome to NXGEN GCXONE! This comprehensive guide will walk you through your first login, account setup, and initial platform configuration. By the end of this tutorial, you'll have a fully configured system ready for production use.

:::tip Video Tutorial Available
Prefer to watch instead of read? Check out our [**4-minute video walkthrough**](/getting-started#watch--learn) that covers everything in this guide.
:::

## What You'll Learn

In this guide, you'll learn how to:

- ✅ Create and register your NXGEN GCXONE account
- ✅ Set up password and security settings
- ✅ Configure user roles and permissions
- ✅ Create your first customer, site, and device
- ✅ Navigate the platform configuration interface

**Estimated Time**: 15-20 minutes

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A valid email address for account registration
- Administrative access credentials (if applicable)
- Your IP camera details (if connecting existing cameras)
- Network information for device integration

---

## Step 1: Account Registration

### 1.1 Create Your Account

Start by navigating to the NXGEN GCXONE registration page and click **Register** to create a brand new account.



![Start by selecting Register to create a brand new account](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_358fe55e1f.png)

*Figure 1: Registration screen - Click Register to begin*


### 1.2 Enter Your Personal Information

Fill in your personal details including name, email address, and contact information. All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.



![Begin by entering your details](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_6f0dd9a688.png)

*Figure 2: User details entry form*


### 1.3 Configure Organization Settings

Enter your organization or company name. This will be used throughout the platform for identification and reporting purposes.



![Enter your organization or company name](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_4fdcca4c52.png)

*Figure 3: Organization name configuration*


---

## Step 2: Camera Setup Options

NXGEN GCXONE offers two flexible setup paths depending on your needs:

### 2.1 Option A: Connect Existing Camera

Choose **Existing Camera** if you want to link your own IP camera for seamless integration with the platform.



![Choose Existing Camera if you want to link your own IP camera for seamless integration](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_51e6b333cb.png)

*Figure 4: Existing camera integration option*


:::info When to Choose This Option
- You have existing IP cameras deployed
- You want to integrate with current infrastructure
- You need full control over camera specifications
:::

### 2.2 Option B: Instant Setup (Demo Mode)

Select **Instant Setup** for a hassle-free demo experience without requiring physical hardware. Perfect for testing and evaluation.



![Prefer quick setup? Select Instant setup for a hassle-free demo without hardware](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_b3b7e7d4f3.png)

*Figure 5: Instant setup option for quick demo*


:::tip Recommended for New Users
Instant Setup is ideal for first-time users who want to explore the platform's capabilities before hardware deployment.
:::

### 2.3 Complete Initial Setup

After making your selections, click **Continue** to proceed with the onboarding process.



![Continue after your selections to proceed with onboarding](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_16258c0d27.png)

*Figure 6: Continue to next step*


---

## Step 3: Finalize Registration

### 3.1 Submit Registration

Click **Register** to finish creating your account and unlock your platform access. You will receive a password reset email to complete the setup.



![Click Register to finish creating your account and unlock your platform access. You will receive a password reset email to set up your password](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_391533ac02.png)

*Figure 7: Complete registration and email confirmation*


:::warning Important
Check your email inbox (and spam folder) for the password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.
:::

### 3.2 First Login

Once you've set up your password through the email link, return to the login page and enter your credentials.



![Login once you set up your password](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_c7a1f8ab36.png)

*Figure 8: Login screen*

Use the account information panel to edit and manage account details. This panel appears at every level of the hierarchy for easy access.

## Step 5: User Management & Permissions

### 5.1 Access Settings

Navigate to **Settings** to configure user roles, permissions, and access controls.



![Settings](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_6ce3c6c695.png)

*Figure 11: Settings menu*


### 5.2 Manage User Roles

View and manage user roles to control platform permissions. NXGEN GCXONE offers granular role-based access control (RBAC).



![View and manage user roles to control platform permissions](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_15e7a6bfc0.png)

*Figure 12: User roles management interface*


### 5.3 Configure Custom Roles

Click **CONFIGURE NEW ROLE** to create custom roles with specific permissions tailored to your organization's needs.



![Click CONFIGURE NEW ROLE to add and customize user access](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_458f27db17.png)

*Figure 13: Configure new role button*


:::tip Role-Based Access Control
Create roles that align with your organizational structure: Admin, Manager, Operator, Viewer, etc. Each role can have different permissions for viewing, editing, and managing resources.
:::

### 5.4 Switch to User Management

Navigate to the **Users** tab to manage user accounts and their associated permissions.



![Switch to Users to manage user accounts and permissions](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_08d6593c64.png)

*Figure 14: Users tab navigation*


### 5.5 Invite Team Members

Click **Invite New User** to bring team members on board and grant them appropriate access levels.



![Use Invite New User to bring team members on board](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_0c79556600.png)

*Figure 15: Invite new user interface*


### 5.6 Assign User Roles

Select the appropriate role for each new user based on their responsibilities and required access level.



![Select the role you want them to have](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_7175c0d0ec.png)

*Figure 16: Role selection for new users*

## Step 6: Customer Configuration

### 6.1 Return to Configuration

Navigate back to the configuration panel to set up your first customer in the platform hierarchy.



![Back to configuration to set up our first customer](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_788c58f3c1.png)

*Figure 17: Return to configuration panel*


### 6.2 Understanding the Hierarchy

NXGEN GCXONE uses a three-tier hierarchy: **Customer → Site → Device**. This structure allows for efficient organization and management of large-scale deployments.



![Customer is the level you will start with, followed by site, and device](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_5f5e114cec.png)

*Figure 18: Three-tier hierarchy structure*


:::info Hierarchy Breakdown
- **Customer**: Top-level organization or client
- **Site**: Physical location or facility under a customer
- **Device**: Individual cameras, sensors, or equipment at a site
:::

### 6.3 Create Customer Profile

Provide the customer name for personalized service records and reporting.



![Provide the customer name for personalized service records](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_d800296414.png)

*Figure 19: Customer name entry*


### 6.4 Enter Customer Details

Fill in complete address information and contact details for the customer profile.



![Fill in the address information and contact details](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_ec5fbaf7c0.png)

*Figure 20: Customer contact information form*


### 6.5 Save Customer Profile

Click **Save** to instantly register your new customer details in the system.



![Save to instantly register your new customer details](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_be63042d4f.png)

*Figure 21: Save customer button*


---

## Step 7: Site Configuration

### 7.1 Select Customer

Click on the customer you just created to begin adding sites under their profile.



![Select the customer we created to add a site under it](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_12379c39e6.png)

*Figure 22: Select customer from list*


### 7.2 Navigate to Sites

Access the site management interface for the selected customer.



![Site management interface](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_dee55341bc.png)

*Figure 23: Site management panel*


### 7.3 Add New Site

Click **Add New** to create a new site under the customer's account.



![Click Add New to create a new site](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_d143a12969.png)

*Figure 24: Add new site button*


### 7.4 Name Your Site

Enter a descriptive name for your site to ensure clear identification and efficient management.



![Name your new site for clear identification and management](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_2e6fb8a3e1.png)

*Figure 25: Site name entry field*


:::tip Naming Best Practices
Use clear, descriptive names like "Headquarters Building", "Warehouse 3", or "Downtown Office" rather than generic names like "Site 1".
:::

### 7.5 Save Site Configuration

Click **Save** to add this site to your customer's portfolio.



![Save to quickly add this site to your customer's portfolio](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_12704e5d4e.png)

*Figure 26: Save site configuration*

## Step 8: Device Integration

### 8.1 Select Site

Click on a site to begin adding devices for monitoring and management.



![Select a site to add a device under it](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_f871b83f91.png)

*Figure 27: Select site from customer list*


### 8.2 Access Device Management

Navigate to the device management interface for the selected site.



![Device management interface](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_e07750c652.png)

*Figure 28: Device management panel*


### 8.3 Add New Device

Click **Add New** to register a new device (camera, sensor, etc.) for this site.



![Click Add New to register a new device for this site](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_42273b4730.png)

*Figure 29: Add new device button*


### 8.4 Select Device Manufacturer

Choose your device manufacturer from the supported list. NXGEN GCXONE supports all major brands including Hikvision, Dahua, Hanwha, and more.



![Choose your device manufacturer](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_0e08388249.png)

*Figure 30: Manufacturer selection dropdown*


:::info Supported Manufacturers
NXGEN GCXONE supports 100+ device manufacturers. If your device isn't listed, contact support for custom integration options.
:::

### 8.5 Configure Device Details

View the manufacturer-specific configuration options.



![Manufacturer-specific options](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_44b4878991.png)

*Figure 31: Device configuration options*


### 8.6 Device Discovery

Enter the device connection details (IP address, port, credentials) and click **Discover** to automatically detect and configure the device.



![Add the device details then click discover](/img/getting-started/first-time-login/img_1531f175db.png)

*Figure 32: Device discovery interface*


:::tip Auto-Discovery
The Discover function automatically detects device capabilities, stream configurations, and available features, simplifying the setup process.
:::

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem**: Password reset email not received
**Solution**: Check spam folder, verify email address, request new reset link

**Problem**: Device discovery fails
**Solution**: Verify network connectivity, check device credentials, ensure device is on same network

**Problem**: Cannot create customer/site
**Solution**: Verify you have appropriate permissions, check for required fields
