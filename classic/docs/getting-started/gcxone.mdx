## Platform Capabilities

### Alarm Management

Real-time alarm processing with intelligent pre-filtering, prioritization, and automated routing. Every alarm includes video verification and a complete audit trail.

- **Smart prioritization** based on threat level and site importance
- **Automated escalation** with configurable workflows
- **Video verification** with instant clip sharing

<Link to="/alarm-management" style={{ display: 'inline-flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '0.5rem', fontWeight: 500 }}>
  Learn about Alarm Management <ArrowRight size={16} />
</Link>

### Device Integration

Connect any alarm panel, camera, or IoT device through our virtual receiver technology—no proprietary hardware required.

- Support for **200+ device manufacturers**
- SIA DC-09 and Contact ID protocol support
- Secure cloud connection without port forwarding

<Link to="/docs/getting-started/devices/hikvision/overview" style={{ display: 'inline-flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '0.5rem', fontWeight: 500 }}>
  Browse Device Guides <ArrowRight size={16} />
</Link>

### Analytics & Reporting

Real-time dashboards with customizable KPIs, historical trend analysis, and automated compliance reports.

- Device health monitoring with predictive alerts
- Operator performance metrics
- Exportable audit reports
