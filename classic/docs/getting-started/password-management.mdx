# 🔐 Password Management

Maintaining a secure password is your first line of defense. This guide covers how to reset a forgotten password and the standards for creating a strong, secure credential.

## 🔄 Resetting Your Password

If you’ve forgotten your password, follow these steps to regain access:

<Steps>

### 1. Access Login
Go to the GCXONE login page and click **Forgot Password?** below the sign-in button.

### 2. Enter Email
Provide the email address associated with your account. Click **Continue**.

### 3. Check Inbox
Look for an email from `@nxgen.cloud` containing a secure reset link. 
<Callout type="info">
The reset link expires after **24 hours**. If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder.
</Callout>

### 4. Create New Password
Click the link and enter your new password twice to confirm. 

</Steps>

---

## 🛡️ Password Standards

To protect your account and your clients' data, GCXONE enforces the following requirements:

- **Length:** Minimum 8 characters (12+ recommended).
- **Complexity:** Must include Uppercase, Lowercase, Numbers, and Special Characters (!@#$%^&*).
- **Uniqueness:** Cannot be a password you have used recently.

### Best Practices
- **Use a Passphrase:** Combine 3-4 random words (e.g., `Sunset-Camera-Blue-99!`).
- **Use a Manager:** Use tools like LastPass or 1Password to generate and store random strings.
- **Enable MFA:** Multi-Factor Authentication is highly recommended for all administrative accounts.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

| Issue | Resolution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **No Email Received** | Wait 5 minutes; check spam; verify the email spelling. |
| **Link Expired** | Request a new reset link from the login page. |
| **Account Locked** | After 5 failed attempts, your account is locked for 30 minutes for security. |
