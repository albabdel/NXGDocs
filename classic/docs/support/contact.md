# Contact Support

Need help? We're here for you.

## Support Channels

### Emergency Support
For critical incidents requiring immediate attention:
- **24/7 Emergency Line**: Contact your account manager for the emergency support number
- **Priority Email**: emergency@nxgen.com

### Standard Support

#### Email
- **General Inquiries**: support@nxgen.com
- **Technical Issues**: techsupport@nxgen.com

#### Online
- **Support Portal**: [Submit a ticket through our support portal](#)
- **Documentation**: Browse our [documentation](/docs) for guides and troubleshooting

## Before Contacting Support

To help us resolve your issue faster, please have the following ready:

1. **Account Information**: Your tenant/customer/site ID
2. **Device Details**: Affected device IDs or serial numbers
3. **Error Messages**: Any error codes or messages you've seen
4. **Steps Taken**: What you've already tried

## Support Hours

- **Standard Support**: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (local time)
- **Emergency Support**: 24/7/365

## Response Times

| Priority | Response Time |
|----------|---------------|
| Critical | 1 hour |
| High | 4 hours |
| Medium | 1 business day |
| Low | 3 business days |
