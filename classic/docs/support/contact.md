# Contact Support

Need help with NXGEN GCXONE? Our support team is here to assist you.

## Submit a Support Ticket

To submit a support request, contact your NXGEN account manager or reach out through the support portal provided during your onboarding.

## Before You Contact Support

To speed up resolution, please have the following information ready:

- Your organization name and account ID
- The GCXONE version you are running
- A description of the issue and steps to reproduce it
- Any relevant screenshots or log excerpts
